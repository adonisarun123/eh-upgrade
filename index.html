⚠️ Risk Assessment & Mitigation

Key Risk Areas & Practical Mitigation

🔴 Primary Risk: Provider Resistance to New Model

Risk: Current providers resist using technology platform

Likelihood: High (60% of providers may resist initially)

Mitigation Strategy:

  • Hybrid approach - maintain traditional alongside new model
  • Start with tech-savvy 20% of providers as champions
  • Comprehensive training + smartphone support
  • Income guarantee during transition period
  • Success stories and peer influence

Budget: ₹8 lakhs for training and incentives

🟡 Secondary Risk: Customer Adoption Speed

Risk: Existing customers slow to adopt new booking method

Likelihood: Medium (customers may prefer familiar process)

Mitigation Strategy:

  • Maintain traditional booking option
  • Personal onboarding for existing customers
  • App benefits demonstration and training
  • Incentives for early platform adoption

Budget: ₹5 lakhs for customer support and incentives

🟢 Low Risk: Technology Platform Issues

Risk: App crashes or technical problems during rollout

Likelihood: Low (simple platform, gradual rollout)

Mitigation Strategy:

  • Start with basic features, add complexity gradually
  • Extensive testing with small user groups
  • Backup traditional systems during transition
  • Local tech support team

Budget: ₹3 lakhs for testing and support
