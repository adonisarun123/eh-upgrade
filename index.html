⚠️ Comprehensive Risk Analysis & Fallback Strategies
High-Risk Scenarios & Mitigation Plans

🔴 Critical Risk: Technology Platform Development Failure

Probability: 25% | Impact: High (₹5 Cr revenue loss in Year 3-4)

Risk Details: Uber platform development faces major delays, cost overruns, or technical failures

Prevention Strategies:
  • Hire experienced tech team with platform development background
  • Partner with proven technology vendor for core platform
  • Implement agile development with monthly milestones
  • Allocate 25% buffer in technology budget (₹75 L extra)
Fallback Plan:
  • Option A: Continue with 4 models, target ₹22 Cr instead of ₹30 Cr
  • Option B: License existing platform (Swiggy/Zomato model)
  • Option C: Focus on geographic expansion of proven models
  • Financial Impact: Revenue reduction of 25%, but still profitable

🟡 Medium Risk: International Market Entry Complications

Probability: 40% | Impact: Medium (₹2 Cr revenue loss)

Risk Details: Regulatory changes, partnership failures, or cultural adaptation issues

Fallback Plan:
  • Focus on domestic premium market instead
  • Redirect international team to high-end domestic placements
  • Target ₹4 Cr from premium domestic vs ₹6 Cr international
  • Maintain overall ₹30 Cr target through other model acceleration

🟡 Medium Risk: Economic Downturn Impact

Probability: 30% | Impact: Medium (15-20% revenue impact)

Risk Details: Economic recession affecting discretionary spending on domestic services

Fallback Plan:
  • Shift focus to essential services (traditional placement)
  • Introduce budget service tiers
  • Accelerate male retail model (recession-resistant)
  • Reduce team size by 20% if needed
  • Target ₹24 Cr instead of ₹30 Cr

🟢 Low Risk: Competition Intensification

Probability: 60% | Impact: Low (5-10% margin pressure)

Risk Details: New players enter market or existing players expand aggressively

Mitigation Strategy:
  • Focus on service quality differentiation
  • Build strong customer loyalty programs
  • Accelerate technology adoption for efficiency
  • Strengthen brand positioning and marketing
