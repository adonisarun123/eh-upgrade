⚠️ Risk Assessment & Mitigation

Key Risk Areas & Bootstrap-Friendly Solutions

🔴 High Risk: Provider Technology Adoption

Challenge: 95% of current providers may struggle with smartphone/app usage

Impact: Could delay transformation and reduce service quality

Bootstrap Mitigation:

  • Start with most tech-savvy 20% of providers (1,000 providers)
  • Intensive training program - ₹500 per provider investment
  • Hybrid model maintains manual dispatch for reluctant providers
  • Peer mentoring system - successful providers train others
  • Gradual transition over 12 months, not forced adoption

Budget: ₹5 lakhs for training and support

🟡 Medium Risk: Customer Migration

Challenge: Existing customers may prefer traditional placement model

Impact: Slow adoption could delay revenue growth

Bootstrap Mitigation:

  • Maintain traditional service alongside new platform
  • Offer app-based customers 10% discount incentive
  • Demonstrate convenience through pilot with willing customers
  • Word-of-mouth marketing from satisfied platform users
  • No forced migration - gradual organic adoption

Budget: ₹2 lakhs for customer incentives

🟡 Medium Risk: Technology Development Delays

Challenge: App development may take longer or cost more than planned

Impact: Could delay launch and increase development costs

Bootstrap Mitigation:

  • Start with MVP approach - basic booking and provider management
  • Use existing website as interim platform while app develops
  • Partner with local development agency for cost-effective solution
  • Phase feature rollout - launch basic version, add features iteratively
  • WhatsApp-based booking as temporary bridge solution

Budget: ₹5 lakhs contingency for development issues

🛡️ Bootstrap Advantage: Lower financial risk allows for gradual, sustainable transformation without pressure for immediate massive growth
